About Bowersox & Bailey

Kenneth Bowersox, Jr. is an artist driven by a passion for family, heritage, and innovation in a world increasingly shaped by cyberspace. His work bridges tradition and modern creativity, blending hands-on craftsmanship with forward-thinking design. He writes novels, designs and develops original games, and creates fresh, artisanal pieces that reflect both creativity and purpose. Each project—whether a story, a game, or a crafted object—is thoughtfully designed to bring meaning, originality, and character into any home or office. Bailey is the craftsman behind the molds, bringing resin to life through experience, curiosity, and innovation. With a passion for creating one-of-a-kind resin products, his work embraces both tradition and experimentation—each piece shaped by thoughtful design and a willingness to explore new techniques. Grounded in a “nothing beats a classic” mindset, Bailey balances time-tested methods with an open-minded approach to cutting-edge innovation. The result is resin work that feels both familiar and forward-thinking, defined by quality, character, and originality.
